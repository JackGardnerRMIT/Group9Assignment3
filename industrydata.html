Industry Data

This page is about the groups prefered jobs in relation to the industry.

What are the Job Titles for your group's ideal jobs? How do each of these rank in terms of demand from employers?

The five job titles which each of the group members is as follows: Jack wishes to be a Penetration tester which is essentially an information technologies security engineer/expert, Eren wishes to become a stockbroker/financial adviser, Phuong wishes to become a Data analyst, Jeff an Information technologies consultant and lastly Majeed an IOS developer.

Using data provided by the Labour Market Information Portal the projected demand in employment growth to May 2024 is as follows.

  1. Database and Systems Administrators, and ICT Security Specialists (Jack & Phuong) = 18.3% growth
  2. Financial Brokers and Dealers, and Investment Advisers (Eren) = 6.9%
  3. Business and Systems Analysts, and Programmers (Jeff) = 20.2%
  4. Software and Applications Programmers (Majeed) = 23.4%

In terms of ranking Majeed's ideal job ranks the highest in terms of growth and demand, followed by Jeff's, then Jack and Phuong and lastly Eren.
