Ideal Jobs

There was a consensus of which career path members of our group wished to pursue, almost all of use wanted to work a job in an IT related path; except for Eren who wants to work as an investment advisor, stockbroker, and financial planner.

Jack

Jack’s ideal job is working in IT as a penetration tester; penetration testers are ethical hackers who are employed by companies to check for exploits and security flaws in IT systems. This job is focused on cyber security, computer protocol and programming. Some of the key skills required for this job include communication, report writing, programming and web and protocol knowledge.

Eren

Eren’s ideal job as previously stated is as an investment advisor, stockbroker, and financial planner; this is very different to the other areas of IT that other group members want. This would involve selling products worth client’s investment, making financial recommendations, tracking in the stock market, and possibly bookkeeping and administration work.

Phuong

Phuong’s ideal job is back to the IT industry specializing in data science. A job in data science would involve collecting data, processing it into useable information using programming languages such as Python and/or R; once the data has been processed into usable information it can be written into detailed reports for companies and organizations to use.

Jeff

Jeff’s ideal job is also in the IT field as an IT consultant. This will include solving IT issues in Windows, Office 365, networking, connectivity and more. Some of the skills required for this include teamwork, time management, communication, knowledgeable and detailed. Also, in the IT field is Majeed’s ideal job as an iOS developer. This job would include designing and then creating iOS applications.

Majeed

Majeed intends to work for his own business. Some of the skills required for this include creativity, artistic mind, hard work and programming. Overall, most of the group members wanted to enter the field of IT, most of the skills and experience we need are very similar. This means that certain areas we can work quite well on, whereas others we may struggle on.
